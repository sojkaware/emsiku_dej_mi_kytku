# Introduction

Hello, adventurers! This is Tripo’s API documentation.

Tripo is designed to transform text and image inputs (i.e., prompts) into high-fidelity 3D models. It provides developers with a flexible, efficient, and scalable solution to incorporate 3D model generation into applications and services.

For those eager to explore our capabilities firsthand, we highly recommend starting with our intuitive web application. Simply visit https://www.tripo3d.ai to embark on your journey with ease.

The Tripo API, on the other hand, empowers you with the full potential of the capability of Tripo. Whether you are enhancing user interaction, creating immersive environments, or developing complex simulations, the Tripo API facilitates seamless integration of 3D content into your projects.

This document will guide you through the API’s capabilities, setup instructions, and usage guidelines to ensure smooth implementation and optimal performance.

# Capabilities

If you’re wondering what the Tripo API can do, have a quick glance here.

- Draft model generation: Get immediate inspiration from our fastest models. This feature is particularly useful for quick prototyping and conceptualization, allowing users to explore various designs or perspectives before committing to a detailed model. Both text and image input are acceptable.
- Refine a draft model: Elevate the quality of a previously generated draft model into a more detailed version. This feature enables a seamless transition from conceptual drafts to high-resolution models, streamlining the creative workflow.
- Animate the model: Bring your models to life with our automated animation feature. This cutting-edge functionality allows users to transform static models into dynamic animations, enhancing presentations or digital experiences.
- Stylization and conversion: Customize and convert your models with unparalleled ease. Our service offers unique stylization options, including transforming models into lego-like or voxel-based versions, injecting a creative and playful twist into your projects. Additionally, this feature supports format conversion, enabling you to effortlessly convert your models into popular formats such as USDZ or FBX with various parameters, ensuring compatibility and ease of use across different platforms and applications.
